Princeps Fury

About the Book

The empire teeters on the brink of collapse. War rages across the land, and old alliances are shattered. In the heart of the conflict, two heroes rise to challenge the darkness.

Anya, the fiery warrior, seeks revenge for her fallen family and will stop at nothing to reclaim her birthright.

Kael, the enigmatic mage, battles to control the ancient powers that threaten to consume the world.

Their paths intertwine as they face formidable enemies and make impossible choices. The fate of the empire rests on their shoulders.
